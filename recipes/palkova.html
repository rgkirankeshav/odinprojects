Palkova

Description

Palkova is a milk sweet made by simmering, thickening and reducing milk until thick then sugar,cardamom powder and ghee are added for flavoring.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Use a heavy bottomed vessel for making palkova so that it doesn't burn easily at the bottom. Also make sure to wet the base of the vessel before pouring milk. I used heavy bottomed bronze uruli. Add milk to it.
  2. Keep the flame in low medium but take care to stir often. This is after almost 1 hour. Milk is reduced to almost 1/4th and is thick.
  3. At this stage add sugar and cardamom powder
  4. Add 1 tablespoon ghee. Give a quick mix.
  5. Mix well, it will leaves the sides of the pan but still gooey. Switch off at this stage, after it cools down it may get more thicker. Enjoy Palkova!
